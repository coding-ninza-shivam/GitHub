About Student Performance Analysis

Welcome to the Student Performance Analysis Portal, a project by IITP 2025 students. Our platform empowers students and educators to track academic progress, analyze performance trends, and receive AI-driven insights for improvement.

This platform is built to provide insights into student performance using data-driven strategies. It helps students and educators improve outcomes by identifying patterns in academic and behavioral data.

Using machine learning, students can get predictive feedback, improvement tips, and visual analytics to better guide their preparation.

Project Vision :

  • Personalized dashboards
  • Performance analytics
  • AI-based grade prediction
  • Secure feedback and support

Our mission: Help every student reach their full potential through data-driven guidance and actionable insights.

Empowering students through smart analysis and personalized strategies.
